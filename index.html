Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

What are your operating hours?

We are open Monday through Sunday from 10:00 AM to 9:00 PM. We recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred time slot, especially on weekends.

Can I simply walk-in or do I need an appointment?

While walk-ins are welcome based on availability, we highly recommend booking an appointment in advance to ensure you get your preferred time and stylist. You can book easily through our website or call us directly.

Do you offer nail art services?

Yes! We offer a wide range of nail services including manicure, pedicure, nail extensions, gel polish, and creative nail art designs. Our nail technicians are trained in the latest trends and techniques.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand plans change. Please inform us at least 4 hours before your appointment if you need to cancel or reschedule. Repeated no-shows may require advance payment for future bookings.

Do you use unisex services?

Yes, Kussh Sidana Salon caters to both men and women. We have specialized services for all genders including haircuts, grooming, facials, and more. Our team is trained to provide personalized care for every client.
